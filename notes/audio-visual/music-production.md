# Music Production

Welcome to my collection of music production notes, tips, and techniques gathered from years of creating synthwave and electronic music.

## DAW Setup & Configuration

### Ableton Live Optimization

Essential settings for optimal performance:

- Buffer Size: 512 samples for production, 128 for recording
- Sample Rate: 48kHz for most projects
- CPU Usage: Enable multi-core support
- Warp Mode: Complex Pro for time-stretching

> 💡 **Tip:** Always freeze tracks with heavy processing to save CPU resources!

## Mixing Techniques

### EQ Settings

```text
# Bass EQ Template
High Pass: 30Hz @ 24dB/oct
Low Shelf: +3dB @ 100Hz
Mid Cut: -2dB @ 500Hz (Q: 0.7)
High Shelf: +1dB @ 5kHz

# Synth Lead EQ
High Pass: 200Hz @ 12dB/oct
Presence: +4dB @ 3kHz (Q: 0.5)
Air: +2dB @ 12kHz (shelf)
```

### Sidechain Compression

```text
Threshold: -20dB
Ratio: 4:1
Attack: 0.1ms
Release: 100ms
Knee: Soft
Gain: +3dB
```

> ⚠️ **Warning:** Be careful with over-compression! It can kill the dynamics in your mix.
